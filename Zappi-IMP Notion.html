The opportunity

Of the ~24 hours per IM, the highest remaining manual burden concentrates in three areas: category request management (complex, multi-system, no AI assist yet), kickoff prep (strong templating candidate), and solution pairing (high back-and-forth, good candidate for guided recommendation). Audience work, status updates, and handover documentation are already AI-assisted.

AI in implementation

A living record of where we use AI today and where we're headed. Updated as we add new capabilities.

Active — what we use today
Sales materials & Gong summarizationPhase 1 — Pre-kickoff
Before every kickoff, AI synthesizes the account plan, sales notes, and Gong call recordings into a structured customer brief. Replaces manual review of scattered documents across SF, email, and Gong.
Impact: faster kickoff prep, more consistent customer context, less time spent hunting information across systems.
Account update GPTPhases 2–6 — recurring
Takes call summaries from active implementation phases and produces a standardized update formatted for the customer's Slack channel. Removes the manual step of translating call notes into comms after every touchpoint.
Impact: reduces the recurring status update burden across all five active phases. Improves consistency of what internal stakeholders receive after each milestone.
Audience auditorPhase 3 — Async collaboration
Accepts multiple customer audience files and assesses them for overlap and differences, then produces recommendations on how to synthesize them into a clean, non-redundant audience structure.
Impact: directly addresses the highest-effort step in the IM. Reduces back-and-forth by giving customers a concrete recommendation rather than an open-ended conversation.
Handover documentationPhase 7 — Handoff
AI assists in drafting the robust SF record update at the end of implementation — summarizing platform configuration, audience and category decisions, training status, and research pipeline for the CSM.
Impact: reduces the highest single manual burden at the close of every IM. Improves consistency of what CSMs receive at handoff.
